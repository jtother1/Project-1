Instructions

  • Click a letter.
  • If you pick a correct letter in the mystery word it will turn green and appear in the spaces where letters are located in the word.
  • If a letter is wrong it will turn red and you must draw a bodypart of the hangman's body. (Example head, body, one hand, one leg)
  • If player chooses wrong six times the hangman has been created and the player loses.
